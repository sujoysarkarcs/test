ŚB 5.12.12

Transliteration

Total Translation & Mapping:

My dear King Rahūgaṇa 1, unless one has the opportunity to smear his entire body 18 with the dust of the lotus feet 17 of great devotees 16, one can not realize the Absolute Truth. One can not realize the Absolute Truth simply by observing celibacy 12[ brahmacarya], strictly following the rules and regulations of householder life 9, leaving home as a vānaprastha, accepting sannyāsa 8, or undergoing severe penances in winter 3 by keeping oneself 14 submerged in water 14 or surrounding oneself in summer by fire and the scorching heat of the sun 14. There are many other processes to understand the Absolute Truth, but the Absolute Truth is only revealed 4 to one who has attained the mercy of a great devotee 16.
