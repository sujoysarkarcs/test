ŚB 10.18.14

Transliteration

kvacid bilvaiḥ kvacit kumbhaiḥ
kvacāmalaka-muṣṭibhiḥ
aspṛśya-netra-bandhādyaiḥ
kvacin mṛga-khagehayā

Total Translation & Mapping:

Sometimes 1 the cowherd boys would play with bilva or 2 kumbha fruits 4, and sometimes 3 with handfuls of āmalaka fruits 6. At other times they would play the games 7 of trying to touch one another 7 or of trying to identify somebody while one is blindfolded 8, and sometimes 10 they would imitate animals 11 and birds 12.
