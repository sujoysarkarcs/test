ŚB 11.22.24

Transliteration

ekādaśatva ātmāsau
mahā-bhūtendriyāṇi ca
aṣṭau prakṛtayaś caiva
puruṣaś ca navety atha

Total Translation & Mapping:

Counting eleven, there are the soul 2, the gross elements 4 and 6 the senses 5. Eight 7 gross and 12 subtle elements plus 8 the Supreme Lord 11 would make nine 13.
