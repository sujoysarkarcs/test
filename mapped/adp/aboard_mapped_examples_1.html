ŚB 8.24.42

Transliteration

dhyāyan bhagavad-ādeśaṁ
dadṛśe nāvam āgatām
tām āruroha viprendrair
ādāyauṣadhi-vīrudhaḥ

Total Translation & Mapping:

As Satyavrata remembered 1 the order of the Supreme Personality of Godhead 2, he saw 3 a boat 4 coming near 5 him. Thus he collected herbs 10 and creepers 11, and, accompanied by saintly brāhmaṇas 8, he got 7 aboard the boat 6.
