ŚB 11.28.24

Transliteration

nātmā vapuḥ pārthivam indriyāṇi
devā hy asur vāyur jalam hutāśaḥ
mano ’nna-mātraṁ dhiṣaṇā ca sattvam
ahaṅkṛtiḥ khaṁ kṣitir artha-sāmyam

Total Translation & Mapping:

The material body 3 made of earth 4 is not 1 the true self 2; nor are the senses 5, their presiding demigods or 6 the air of life 8; nor is the external air 9, water 10 or fire 11 or one’s mind 12. All these are simply matter 13. Similarly, neither one ’s intelligence 14, material consciousness 16 nor ego 17, nor the elements of ether 18 or earth 19, nor the objects of sense perception 20, nor even the primeval state of material equilibrium 21 can be considered the actual identity of the soul.
