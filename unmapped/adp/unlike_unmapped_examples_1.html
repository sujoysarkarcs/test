ŚB 6.2.11

Transliteration

na niṣkṛtair uditair brahma-vādibhis
tathā viśuddhyaty aghavān vratādibhiḥ
yathā harer nāma-padair udāhṛtais
tad uttamaśloka-guṇopalambhakam

Total Translation & Mapping:

By following 8 the Vedic ritualistic ceremonies or undergoing atonement 2, sinful men 7 do not become as purified as 6 by chanting once 12 the holy name 11 of Lord Hari 10. Although ritualistic atonement may free one from sinful reactions, it does not awaken devotional service, unlike the chanting of the Lord ’s names 11, which reminds one 16 of the Lord ’s fame, qualities 15, attributes, pastimes and paraphernalia.
