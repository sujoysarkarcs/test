ŚB 8.7.13

Transliteration

upary adhaś cātmani gotra-netrayoḥ
pareṇa te prāviśatā samedhitāḥ
mamanthur abdhiṁ tarasā madotkaṭā
mahādriṇā kṣobhita-nakra-cakram

Total Translation & Mapping:

The demigods and demons worked almost madly for the nectar, encouraged by the Lord, who was above and below the mountain and who had entered 7 the demigods, the demons 3, Vāsuki and 4 the mountain itself. Because of the strength of the demigods 11 and demons, the Ocean of Milk 10 was 14 so powerfully agitated 14 that all the alligators in the water 15 were very much perturbed. Nonetheless the churning of the ocean 9 continued in this way.
