ŚB 11.3.21

Transliteration

tasmād guruṁ prapadyeta
jijñāsuḥ śreya uttamam
śābde pare ca niṣṇātaṁ
brahmaṇy upaśamāśrayam

Total Translation & Mapping:

Therefore 1 any person who seriously desires real happiness must seek a bona fide spiritual master 2 and take shelter of 3 him by initiation. The qualification of the bona fide guru is that he has realized the conclusions of the scriptures by deliberation and is able to convince others of these conclusions. Such great personalities, who have taken shelter of the Supreme Godhead 7, leaving aside all material considerations 11, should 3 be understood to be bona fide spiritual masters 2.
