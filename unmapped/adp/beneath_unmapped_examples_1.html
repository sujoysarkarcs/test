ŚB 10.25.22

Transliteration

tathā nirviviśur gartaṁ
kṛṣṇāśvāsita-mānasaḥ
yathāvakāśaṁ sa-dhanāḥ
sa-vrajāḥ sopajīvinaḥ

Total Translation & Mapping:

Their minds 6 thus 1 pacified 5 by Lord Kṛṣṇa 4, they all entered 2 beneath the hill, where they found ample room for themselves and all their cows 8, wagons 9, servants and priests 10, and for all other members of the community as 10 well 7.
