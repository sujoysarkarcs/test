ŚB 12.6.5

Transliteration

bhagavaṁs takṣakādibhyo
mṛtyubhyo na bibhemy aham
praviṣṭo brahma nirvāṇam
abhayaṁ darśitaṁ tvayā

Total Translation & Mapping:

My lord 1, I now have no fear 5 of Takṣaka or 2 any other living 3 being, or even of repeated deaths 4, because I have absorbed myself in 8 that purely spiritual Absolute Truth 8, which you have revealed and which destroys all fear.

Glossary:

1. bhagavan — my lord;   2. takṣaka — from the snake-bird Takṣaka;   3. ādibhyaḥ — or other living entities;   4. mṛtyubhyaḥ — from repeated deaths;   5. na bibhemi — do not fear;   6. aham — I;   7. praviṣṭaḥ — having entered;   8. brahma — the Absolute Truth;   9. nirvāṇam — exclusive of everything material;   10. abhayam — fearlessness;   11. darśitam — shown;   12. tvayā — by you.;
