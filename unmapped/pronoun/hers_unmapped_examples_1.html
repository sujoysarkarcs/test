ŚB 3.22.22

Transliteration

so ’nu jñātvā vyavasitaṁ
mahiṣyā duhituḥ sphuṭam
tasmai guṇa-gaṇāḍhyāya
dadau tulyāṁ praharṣitaḥ

Total Translation & Mapping:

After having unmistakably known 3 the decision 4 of the Queen 5, as well as that of Devahūti, the Emperor 1 most gladly gave 10 his daughter to the sage 6, whose host of virtues 9 was equaled 11 by hers.
